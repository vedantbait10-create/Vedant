About me

Started on mobile.
Now building
real craft.

I'm Vedant — a motion designer and video editor based in Kandivali, Mumbai. I've been editing videos on my phone for years, but six months ago I got my first laptop and started learning professional tools properly. That's when things got serious.

I recently completed a 3-month internship at McCann Reputation Management (MRM), where I created motion graphic videos for national brands like Tata Capital and Nilkamal. That experience taught me how agencies work, how to take a brief and execute it, and how much there is still to learn — which keeps me motivated every day.
